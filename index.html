About Sea & Ski

A New Vision
for French Tourism

CEO of Sea and Ski is Nathan Nicolaou. At only 18 years old, Nathan embodies a new generation of tourism entrepreneurs — dynamic, connected and passionate. Having grown up surrounded by the tourism world, he quickly developed a deep understanding of the industry and built valuable contacts within it.

Currently pursuing studies in marketing, Nathan combines strategic vision with creativity to design travel experiences that merge authenticity, adventure and modernity.

Motivated by his passion for discovery from the sea to the mountains, he founded Sea and Ski — a company that offers unique and sustainable tourism experiences inspired by both nature and innovation.

Despite his young age, Nathan has already surrounded himself with an experienced and motivated team, sharing a common goal: to bring a fresh and modern perspective to tourism while emphasizing quality, respect for the environment, and human connection.

🌊 Driven · Curious · Ambitious — Every journey is a story to be written
